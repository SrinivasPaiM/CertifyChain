User Guide

How to Use CertifyChain

Welcome to the CertifyChain user guide. Follow these steps to generate and verify refugee certificates.

Step 1: Login as Issuer

Access the admin panel by clicking on "Login" in the navigation menu. Use your credentials to log in.

Step 2: Generate a Certificate

Navigate to "Generate Certificate" and fill in the refugee's details:

  • Recipient Address (Ethereum wallet address)
  • Refugee Name
  • Country of Origin
  • Date of Birth
  • Address
  • Gender
  • Validity Period

Click "Submit" to issue the certificate on the blockchain.

Step 3: Verify a Certificate

Anyone can verify a certificate by entering the Certificate ID on the homepage. The system will check both the database and the blockchain.

Step 4: Download PDF

After generating a certificate, you can download a PDF version with a QR code for easy verification.
