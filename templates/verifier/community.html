About CertifyChain

CertifyChain is a blockchain-powered system designed to help refugees obtain secure, tamper-proof digital certificates. Our mission is to empower refugees by providing them with verifiable credentials that can help them access education, healthcare, and employment opportunities.

Why Blockchain?

Blockchain technology offers several advantages for certificate management:

  • Immutability: Once certificates are issued, they cannot be altered
  • Transparency: All transactions are publicly verifiable
  • Decentralization: No single point of failure
  • Security: Cryptographic protection against tampering
