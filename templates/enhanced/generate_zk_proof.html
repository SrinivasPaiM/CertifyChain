What is a ZK Proof?

Zero-Knowledge Proof lets you prove something is true WITHOUT revealing the actual information.

Example: Instead of showing your ID card to prove you're eligible for healthcare, you can generate a mathematical proof that says "I am eligible" without showing any personal details.
